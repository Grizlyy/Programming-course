Built with love

Super Gym’s founder, Jim O’Hogen, opened his unique fitness center in February 2006, after struggling to find a facility that met his demanding expectations. “The fitness industry is constantly being devalued due to poor management and a serious lack of true professionals, which lowers overall value and members expectations of their fitness experience. My staff and I are constantly looking for ways to improve our members experience, from innovative training techniques to an immaculate, non- intimidating atmosphere”.
